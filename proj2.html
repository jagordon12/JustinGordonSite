Project Overview

In Computer Science 309 we created an idea for a teachers social hub. This hub would be used for teachers to share course curriculum and give feedback to one another in different aspects. This webpage also included virtual classrooms and a way to follow other teachers or join their classes. We included email verification, profiles, and teacher and student roles for which classroom you were visiting.
