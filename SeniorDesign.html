Project Overview

For our senior design project we worked in conjunction with a Iowa State University researcher, Dr. Liang Dong. The goal of this project was to create a Ultra-Thin Wearable for Real-Time Health Monitoring. Working with a team of 5 including another software engineer and 3 electrical engineers we are tasked with developing a band-aid like sensor used for monitoring the health of its user.

Big Picture Contribution

The big picture contribution for this project is to make health monitoring more accessible for the general market. This will allow for people to have easier access to cheaper real-time health monitoring devices that are smaller and more comfortable.
